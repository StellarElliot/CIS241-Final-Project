Hello, my name is Elliot Blaufuss, I am a relatively new student to the Web design space. Although I haven't been learning HTML andd CSS for very long, I am starting to feel more comfortable using them to create and format Web pages. Figuring out new creative ways to design a site using HTML and CSS can actually be pretty fun. After this class, I look forward to expanding my knowledge on the subject even further!

I started attending Edmonds Community College in the Fall of 2017 and am going to be finished taking classes here after this quarter. When I graduate, I am going to transfer to The University of Washington Bothell and pursue a degree in video design and animation. I am excited to continue my education and take more classes in my field! Hopefully the transition is both easy and smooth!

Before moving to Lynnwood, Washington for college, I lived in a smaller town in Washington called Wenatchee. Wenatchee is about 130 miles to the East of Lynnwood. Living over there was nice, but there was never much to do in such a small town. I think I strongly prefer living Lynnwood because it seems like there is always something going on pretty close by and it is not as difficult to travel to downtown.

In my free time my favorite thing to do is create digital art and animation.
